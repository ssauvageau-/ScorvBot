ScorvBot does **not** collect your information on this server with any permanence beyond Discord itself.
**None** of your information is used by ScorvBot for the purposes of training AI or LLMs.

ScorvBot **does** perform the following:
* Store hashed versions of your messages' contents for a set number of seconds before those hashes expire.
* Catalog your deleted messages, sending their contents to moderator-only channels.
* Track when users connect and disconnect from voice calls. (Voice communication itself is not processed by ScorvBot)
* Catalog when users include masked URLs in their messages, revealing those masks (and the links beneath them) to moderators.
* Logs when users utilize any of its slash-commands or trigger any of its events, such as Thinkematics.

ScorvBot may try to DM you for certain features, such as automated thread deletion or Tag approval/rejection. We encourage users to allow Applications like ScorvBot to message them privately, but we cannot enforce this, and you are free to ignore messages from Discord Applications at your discretion.

By remaining on this server, you consent to the above operations.

As development of ScorvBot continues, this privacy policy may update to reflect additional features.
